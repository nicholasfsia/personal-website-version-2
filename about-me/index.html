Nicholas F. Sia is a Jakarta Toronto-based designer, currently pursuing a degree in Systems Design Engineering at the University of Waterloo. He enjoys designing, playing tennis, running, cooking, and listening to podcasts. His favourite tennis players are Novak Djokovic and Maria Sharapova and favourite podcast is definitely Design Details.
